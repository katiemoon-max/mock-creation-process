## AO3 litmus test (mandatory before classifying any mark as AO3)

Ask these, in order:

1. **Novelty:** Is there information (data, diagram, scenario, claim) the student must interpret that they have not seen in revision?
2. **Engagement:** Does answering *require* engaging with that specific information, or could a memorised answer also satisfy the mark scheme?
3. **Judgement:** Is the student making a judgement, evaluation, or synthesis — not just applying a known procedure?

If any answer is "no", it is NOT AO3. Re-classify as AO1 (recall) or AO2 (application).
